About Anastasia

Lash expertise that
actually sees you

I'm Anastasia — a lash and brow technician with over six years of experience, working from my private home studio in the heart of Stroud.

Most of my clients are women in their 50s and beyond. I've made it my work to understand how lashes and brows change with age: the sparseness, the changes in direction, the sensitivity. I use only the gentlest adhesives — TPO-free, tested for sensitive skin — and take every appointment at the pace you need.

This isn't a conveyor-belt salon. You get my full attention, a quiet space, and results that look polished without looking overdone.

TPO-free adhesives

My glue is free from TPO — a sensitiser now banned across the EU. Kinder on mature, sensitive skin and safer for the long term.

02

Specialist in mature eyes

Thinner lashes, hooded lids, and changed growth patterns need a different approach. I've built my practice around getting this right.

03

Private home studio

No background noise, no rushing. My studio in Stroud is calm, unhurried, and entirely focused on you during your appointment.
